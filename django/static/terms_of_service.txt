Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/12/24

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

	•	Account Information: Username, email address, profile avatar, banner, match history, and description.
	•	Activity Data: Interactions and activities performed while using our services.
	•	Cookies: We use cookies strictly for essential functionality, such as authentication and maintaining your session.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:
	•	Create and manage your account.
	•	Authenticate your access and maintain your session using secure tokens.
	•	Enable and improve game features like friendships and user interactions.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help our web app function. We only use cookies for:
	•	Authentication: To securely verify your identity using JSON Web Tokens (JWTs).
	•	Session Management: To maintain your login session while you use the app.

We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law or to operate the service (e.g., secure hosting).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
	•	Access Your Data: Request a copy of your personal data.
	•	Correct Your Data: Fix any inaccurate or incomplete information.
	•	Delete Your Data: Delete your account, which will remove all associated data within 30 days.
	•	Withdraw Consent: Manage or delete cookies via your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove all associated data within 30 days.
